Services

The following are some of the Working Group Facilities “POKJA” in Lombok Kulon Organic Tourism Village

Human Resources Working Group

Receives tourist visits and is engaged in the improvement of human resources.

Agriculture & Plantation Working Group

The procedure for planting rice and vegetables using organic methods, making organic fertilizer with materials from the surrounding nature, and packaging rice from organic farming.

Fisheries Working Group

Managing fish ponds from hatchery to harvest. Provide information and training on how to grow fish using organic methods.

Attraction Working Group

Giving briefing and briefing before tubing starts. The activity lasts for 1 hour. The guides are certified and spread out at several points to supervise, guard, and take visitor documentation.

Craft Working Group

Make crafts with banana fronds and wood. The results of the craft can be directly brought by visitors who make the craft.

Culinary Working Group

Processing agricultural and fishery products produced by organic tourism villages.

Homestay Working Group

Provide comfortable lodging for visitors who want to stay overnight.
